🎯 Career Aspirations

My goal is to operate at the intersection of technology and business strategy. I want to build a career across three pillars — software development, Revenue Operations (RevOps), and Systems Science. I see these not as separate paths but as three lenses through which I can understand how organisations grow, break down, and fix themselves. Whether I'm building tools, designing revenue funnels, or modelling complex adaptive systems, the thread connecting it all is a fascination with how things work at scale. Ultimately, I want to consult and build for businesses that are growing fast but haven't quite figured out their internal systems yet — that's where I know I can add the most value.
